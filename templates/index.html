About Me

Jared Milos

I had been managing an eCommerce customer service team & running the company's "Voice of the Customer" program, working directly with the site's developers and data engineers. I realized that I could read and edit the SQL queries, and some of the HTML. I decided that instead of just reporting customer issues, I wanted to be able to build solutions for them directly.

My coding journey began with an intense 4-month bootcamp at The Coding Dojo in San Jose, CA, learning three full stacks, dozens of additional frameworks and technologies, common algorithms and data structures. Having built multiple apps and sites by myself and within a team structure, I'm excited to continue to strengthen my skills in a business environment.
